OMSI

Our partnership with OMSI is a valued collaboration with a company that has been a leader in the domestic and international market for more than 60 years. OMSI is a company with tradition, founded in 1961 in Zola Predosa, the industrial area of Bologna, and has a determined and ambitious spirit, which has shown its success over time.

IVARS

Our partnership with IVARS is a valuable collaboration with a company that offers a wide range of components for chairs. Through careful analysis of market needs and detailed studies, IVARS aims to maximize ergonomic comfort and develop solutions that are increasingly strategic and functional. Their goal is to provide rich components and functional sets for different environments to provide real solutions and not just simple products.

Albacomponents

Our partnership with Albacomponents is a valued connection, where we collaborate to provide beautiful, innovative, functional and detailed furniture solutions. Through the carefully coordinated work of all their departments, Albacomponents aims to provide advanced furniture solutions to the market. Their Italian identity and the passion of their team are powerful resources for achieving common goals.
